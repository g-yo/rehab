BMW F 900 XR OVERVIEW & PRICE

PERFOMANCE AND ENGINE

The BMW F 900 XR is powered by a robust 895cc, water-cooled, 4-stroke in-line two-cylinder engine, the beating heart of this machine. This powerhouse churns out an impressive 73 kW (97.82 hp) at 8,500 rpm and a torque of 92 Nm at 6,500 rpm, ensuring a thrilling performance across various riding conditions. With its output surpassing many competitors in its class, the F 900 XR is a force to be reckoned with, boasting over 10 hp more than some of its rivals. The latest model has been fine-tuned to deliver a sportier feel in the Dynamic ride mode, further enhancing the bike's performance characteristics. This adjustment allows riders to experience a more engaging ride when desired while still maintaining the versatility that the F 900 XR is known for. The transmission system features a 6-speed constant-mesh gearbox paired with a multi-plate clutch in the oil bath, including a slipper clutch for smoother downshifts. This setup ensures smooth power delivery and contributes to the refined riding experience.

FEATURES AND TECHNOLOGY

The BMW F 900 XR has impressive features that enhance performance and rider comfort. Here's a detailed look at some of the standout features:

  • 1.Advanced Electronics: The BMW F 900 XR has advanced electronics that enhance performance and rider comfort. It features a full-colour TFT display with BMW Motorrad Connectivity, providing easy access to vital information and smartphone integration. The bike also includes Dynamic Traction Control (DTC), Engine Braking Control (EBC), and BMW Motorrad ABS Pro for enhanced safety and control.
  • 2. Lighting:The F 900 XR features adaptive LED headlights with cornering lights, significantly improving visibility during night rides and through curves.
  • 3. Riding Modes: Standard riding modes include Rain, Road, and Dynamic, allowing riders to adjust the bike's performance characteristics to suit different conditions.
  • 4. Suspension: The latest model introduces a new 43mm USD fork with preload, compression, and rebound damping adjustment for the front suspension, while the rear features a mono-shock. This setup provides a balance of comfort and sporty handling.
  • 5. Comfort Features: The bike has standard heated grips and a new windscreen design for improved wind protection. The latest model also includes new handguards and is 3 kg lighter than its predecessor, thanks to new 17-inch wheels that are 1.8 kg lighter.
  • 6. Safety Features: The BMW F 900 XR has advanced safety features to ensure a secure and confident ride. Dynamic brake control (DBC) and an intelligent emergency call system are included, enhancing rider safety. These features provide an added layer of protection, giving riders peace of mind on the road.
  • 7. Convenience: The latest model has added a new standard USB-C charging port, making long rides convenient.

BUILD QUALITY AND DURABILITY

The BMW F 900 XR is renowned for its robust build quality and durability, making it well-suited for the challenging road conditions in India. The bike features a steel bridge-type frame and a cast aluminium swingarm, providing strength and stability. This construction ensures that the F 900 XR can confidently handle diverse riding environments. User reviews and expert opinions praise the F 900 XR for its high-quality construction and materials. While some early users reported concerns about oil consumption, these issues were resolved as the engine broke in over time. Long-term users have reported covering significant distances without major problems, indicating the bike's reliability and durability. Although not the most advanced in its class, the suspension system offers a good balance of control and comfort. This mainly benefits Indian roads, ranging from smooth highways to rough rural paths. The latest model's updates, including the new front fork and lighter wheels, are expected to further enhance the bike's handling and durability.BMW's attention to detail is evident in the F 900 XR's fit and finish, with high-grade materials used throughout. The premium switchgear and overall construction quality contribute to the bike's reputation for reliability, making it a trustworthy companion for daily and long-distance tours.

HANDLING

The BMW F 900 XR is renowned for its nimble handling and agile nature, making it an excellent choice for urban commuting and highway cruising. The bike's handling characteristics are particularly noteworthy. It offers easy low-speed turn-in, which is beneficial for navigating through city traffic. The F 900 XR demonstrates excellent high-speed handling, providing stability and confidence during highway rides. The suspension system, featuring USD forks and single shocks with adjustable rebound, effectively manages bumpy corners, enhancing overall stability and control.

COMFORT AND ERGONOMICS

The BMW F 900 XR is designed with long-distance comfort in mind, although some riders have reported mixed experiences. The bike features an upright and relaxed riding position, which helps reduce rider fatigue during extended journeys. Adjustable seat heights ranging from 775 mm to 870 mm allow riders to customize their seating position for optimal comfort. A flexible two-level windshield is included to enhance wind protection. However, some users have noted issues with wind blasts at highway speeds. The latest model includes a new standard USB-C charging port, adding convenience for long rides. However, it's worth noting that some riders have expressed concerns about the suspension and seat comfort, particularly during longer rides. This suggests that personal preferences and riding styles influence the overall comfort experience.

STYLING AND DESIGN

The BMW F 900 XR boasts a striking design combining sportiness and touring functionality. The bike features a dynamic fairing and a slim, visually short tail, emphasizing its athletic nature. LED headlights with adaptive cornering lights enhance visibility and contribute to the bike's modern aesthetic. The full-colour TFT display with BMW Motorrad Connectivity adds a touch of sophistication to the cockpit. The overall design reflects current trends in automotive styling, including a focus on minimalism and the integration of smart technologies.

It's suitable for city and weekend trips. Still, those prioritizing long-distance touring should be aware of potential comfort issues on longer rides, which might necessitate aftermarket modifications. Overall, the F 900 XR delivers a premium riding experience with cutting-edge technology and dynamic design. It is a solid investment for riders who value performance, style, and the BMW brand as long as it fits their riding needs and budget.
